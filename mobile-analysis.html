Introduction

For this mobile viewing i choose one of the most popular soical media outlets instagram

Questions to consider:

  • Can you find what you're looking for?

Findings

Can you still see what you want to see?

For the most part it is the very similar to the app that you would see on your phone but there are some big differences

Can you find what you're looking for?

You can't find the instagram story bar and you can't view live videos. You can usually browse other live videos but you can't do this on your mobile brower. Also you can't view Saved pictures, pictures of you and you can't scroll though your pictures like you can on the app

Can you do what you want to do?

Aside from the things I mentioned above you can basically do everything else.

Any frustrations?

The bottom navigation bar is not well placed as it is next to my safari navigation so sometimes it opens a new tab when I don't want it to.

Anything work better on the small screen?

Nothing works better than compared to the app or online

Summary

I learned about what these websites cut when using there mobile versions. I learned that even sometimes they cut some features so they aren't even availiable to me. I learned that their apps just work so much better and that using the mobile browser is just very restrictive
